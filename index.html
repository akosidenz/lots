Safety
First

Yes! Your safety comes first.
Your health is important to us and we're taking additional safety measures in store to keep our community safe.

Surface Disinfection

Regular sanitation and disinfection of our carts and shopping baskets

Hand Disinfection

Providing sanitizer for Customers and Employees

Store Disinfection

Additional store cleaning and sanitizing

Face Protection

Requiring all Customers and Employees to wear a face covering

Temperature Check

Requiring Body Temperature Check before store entry

Social Distancing

Encouraging social distancing

Store Limit

Limiting the number of Customers in store at a time

Contactless Payment

Providing contactless payment options

PPE

Providing personal protective equipment to our Employees

Protective Barriers

Plexiglass shields or Plastic barriers at registers

About
Us

Welcome to Lots for Less Warehouse Sale! Incorporated 1997.
We are a home-grown Davao based store dedicated to providing household products at the best prices for value for money.
At Lots for Less, you SAVE MONEY so you can LIVE MORE.

  • Our Vision
  • Our Mission
  • Our Core Values

  • Vision Statement

    To make Lots for Less Warehouse Sale the leading home-grown retail store in Mindanao committed to helping our customers save money and have a better life while giving them excellent service.

  • Mission Statement

      • Customer-Focused Quality Service – We build relationship with our customers by helping our customers save money and giving them a better life while providing excellent customer service.

      • Highly-Valued Business Partners – Our Business partnership is built on trust and we recognize the value and contribution of all our partners.

      • Responsible Citizenship – We support programs that help in the sustainable development of the communities where we operate in.

      • Empowered Employees – Our employees feel excited to work in a family-like environment where they are encouraged to participate, learn and grow while living the values of service, professionalism and integrity.

      • Long-term Returns – We commit to achieving continual growth and good returns of investment for our shareholders.

  • Our Core Values

      1. SERVICE-ORIENTED : We are focused on our customers. We treat them the way we like to be treated.
      "So in everything, do to others what you would have them do to you." - Matthew 7:12

      2. PROFESSIONAL : We make an effort to do our duties well. We accept responsibilities and work on results.
      “Those who work their land will have abundant food, but those who chase fantasies will have their fill of poverty.” - Proverbs 12:11

      3. INTEGRITY : We are sincere, honest and trustworthy. We do the right thing even when nobody is looking.
      "If anyone, then, knows the good they ought to do and doesn’t do it, it is sin for them." - James 4:17

      4. FAMILY-ORIENTED : We are one team. We respect each other and help each other.
      “Behold, how good and how pleasant it is for brothers to dwell together in unity!” - Psalm 133:1

      5. PASSION : We are excited and committed to win. We are determined to improve ourselves and our company.
      "Encourage one another and build each other up." -1 Thessalonians 5:11

      6. ENTREPRENEURIAL SPIRIT : We move fast, we are cost-conscious, we are creative and open-minded.
      “Commit your work to the Lord, and your plans will be established.” – Proverbs 16:3
